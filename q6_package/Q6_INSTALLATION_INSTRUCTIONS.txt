WHAT THIS PAGE SHOWS:
================================================================================

Q6 answers: "What are the top research topics being funded, and which 
institutions excel in which research domains?"

- Top 15 disease topics ranked by grant count and funding
- Top 15 methods topics ranked by grant count and funding
- Institutional domain leadership with log-scale comparisons
- Corewell Health portfolio composition analysis
- Strategic recommendations for competitive positioning

Key Visualizations:
- 4 horizontal bar charts (Top 15 topics by disease/methods)
- 2 grouped bar charts with log scale (institutional comparison)
- 2 heatmaps with log scale (funding intensity)
- 1 dual pie chart (Corewell portfolio breakdown)

Key Insights:
- Neurology and AI/ML dominate research funding
- UPMC is 130x larger than Corewell (requires niche strategy)
- Corewell's relative strengths in Oncology and Organ-Specific domains
- Strategic recommendations for partnership vs. build decisions

DATA NOTES:
================================================================================

The 9 PNG images were generated from CSV data using:
- disease_09_table.csv (31 disease sub-categories)
- disease_10_table.csv (31 disease funding amounts)
- methods_09_table.csv (27 methods sub-categories)
- methods_10_table.csv (27 methods funding amounts)
- disease_02_table.csv (institution x disease grant counts)
- disease_03_table.csv (institution x disease funding)
- methods_02_table.csv (institution x methods grant counts)
- methods_03_table.csv (institution x methods funding)

Visualizations use LOG SCALE for institutional comparisons to make all 
4 institutions visible (otherwise Corewell would be invisible at 1/130th 
of UPMC's scale).
